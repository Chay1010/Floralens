About the Model

Technical details of the FloraLens AI engine

🏗️ Architecture

EfficientNet-B2 with compound scaling, fine-tuned on Oxford 102 Flowers. 7.8M parameters with transfer learning from ImageNet.

📊 Dataset

Oxford 102 Flowers — 8,189 images across 102 species. Stratified 70/15/15 split with aggressive augmentation (Mixup, ColorJitter, RandomErasing).

⚡ Serving

ONNX Runtime via FastAPI. Dockerized deployment with health checks. 12.3ms mean inference latency on GPU.

📈 Performance

89.7% accuracy, 0.412 val loss. Trained with AdamW (lr=3e-4), cosine annealing, and early stopping (patience=5).
